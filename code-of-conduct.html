CODE OF CONDUCT

This document serves as a general overview of
the expected behavior of a StartNG’s intern

WHY STARTNG

StartNG is a friendly place to learn, you gain access to enough knowledge to introduce and aid your success in the tech industry. We’re committed to keeping it that way.


By joining StartNG, you agree that you'll follow this code of conduct.

ANTI-HARRASSMENT AND UNRULY BEHAVIOUR

This includes sexual language and imagery, deliberate intimidation, stalking, name-calling, unwelcome attention, libel, and any malicious hacking or social engineering. freeCodeCamp should be a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, national origin, or religion (or lack thereof).


Unruly behaviour includes posting inflammatory comments to provoke an emotional response or disrupt discussions, posting off-topic messages to disrupt discussions, promoting a product, soliciting donations, advertising a job / internship / gig, or flooding discussions with files or text(This can only be done outside the main chnnels).

DISRUPTIVE CONDUCT

You acknowledge and agree that StartNG reserves the right to remove you from the internship if StartNG, in its sole discretion, determines that your presence or behavior create a disruption or hinder the internship or the enjoyment of the internship by other interns

TASK DUTIES AND AUTHORITY

All interns should submit all task given with integrity and respect toward mentors and the team leads. Mentors and team leads mustn’t abuse their authority. We expect them to delegate duties to their team members taking into account their competences and workload. Likewise, we expect team members to follow team leaders’ instructions and complete their tasks with skill and in a timely manner.

If you see someone harass, troll, or spam anywhere in the StartNG workspace (forum, chat, YouTube, Facebook, etc.), notify us in the  admin channel  - preferably with a screen shot of the offense. The moderator team will take any action we deem appropriate, up to and including banning the offender from StartNG.
